about me

In my career, I have also served as the Chairperson of the Credit Information Center, a member of the Executive Committee of the South East Asian Central Banks Research and Training Centre (SEACEN) in Malaysia, a member of the SAARC Finance Payment Council, an alternate director on the board of the Alliance for Financial Inclusion (AFI), and an editor for the Economic Review published by Nepal Rastra Bank.

Dr Neelam Dhungana Timsina

The accomplishments achieved through the honest and diligent fulfillment of my duties have undeniably propelled the economy forward, encouraged the productive sector, uplifted impoverished and marginalized communities, supported the Government of Nepal's economic development initiatives, and ultimately contributed to Nepal Rastra Bank's success. Witnessing these positive impacts fills me with profound pride
